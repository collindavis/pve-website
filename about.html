Who We Are

Serving Vermont &
New Hampshire

Prosper Valley Electric proudly serves homeowners, businesses and contractors throughout Vermont and New Hampshire with dependable electrical services built on hard work, honesty, and quality craftsmanship.

We understand that electrical work is one of the most important parts of any property. It powers your home, protects your investment, and keeps your daily life moving. That's why we approach every project with professionalism, attention to detail, and pride in doing the job right.

From new construction and remodels to generator installations, service upgrades, EV chargers, and custom lighting, we provide reliable electrical solutions tailored to each customer's needs.

As a local company, we value relationships, trust, and community. Our goal is simple: provide the kind of service people feel confident recommending to family, friends, and neighbors.
