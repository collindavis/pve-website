Who We Are

Built on Reliability,
Quality & Local Pride

Prosper Valley Electric was built around the values that matter most: hard work, honest service, and quality craftsmanship.

As a locally owned company, we understand the importance of dependable electrical systems in your home, business, or job site. We take pride in helping our neighbors and local contractors complete projects safely, efficiently, and correctly the first time.

Whether you're building a new home, upgrading outdated electrical equipment, installing a standby generator, or planning custom lighting for a remodel, we bring experience, attention to detail, and real-world problem solving to every project.

We believe local businesses make communities stronger — and we work hard to earn the trust of every customer we serve.
