Our Electrical
Services

Dependable electrical work for homes, businesses, remodels, and new construction throughout Vermont and New Hampshire.

New Construction Wiring

Reliable electrical installations for new homes, garages, shops, and commercial spaces. We work closely with homeowners and contractors to keep projects moving smoothly from rough-in to final inspection.

  • Complete electrical system installation
  • Service installation
  • Panel setup
  • Interior & exterior lighting
  • Generator prep
  • Smart home & specialty wiring
  • Coordination with builders and contractors

Remodels & Renovations

Electrical upgrades designed to match your vision while improving safety and functionality. We help bring old spaces up to modern standards while keeping installations clean, efficient, and code-compliant.

  • Kitchen remodel wiring
  • Bathroom remodels
  • Basement finishing
  • Additions & renovations
  • Rewiring outdated systems
  • Custom lighting layouts
  • Outlet & switch upgrades

Generator Installation, Sales & Service

Protect your home or business with dependable backup power solutions. We are certified Kohler Home Energy and DuroMax Authorized installers. We help customers choose the right generator setup for their property and provide dependable installation and long-term service support.

  • Whole-home standby generators
  • Portable generator hookups
  • Generator sales
  • Transfer switches
  • Maintenance & troubleshooting
  • Service and repairs

EV Charger Installation

Safe, reliable charging solutions for your home or business. As electric vehicles become more common, we help homeowners and businesses prepare for the future with properly installed charging systems.

  • Level 2 charger installation
  • Dedicated circuits
  • Garage charger setups
  • Outdoor charging stations
  • Load calculations & panel evaluations

Service Installations & Upgrades
