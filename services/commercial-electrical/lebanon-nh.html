Commercial Electrical Services in Lebanon, NH

We serve a wide range of commercial clients in Lebanon:

Tenant Improvements — New circuits, dedicated loads, and lighting for build-outs and renovations. We work directly with contractors, property managers, and business owners.

Lighting Retrofits — LED upgrades for commercial spaces that reduce energy costs and improve light quality. We handle the wiring, fixture selection guidance, and installation.

Panel Upgrades & Service Upgrades — Many commercial properties in Lebanon are running on electrical service that was sized for a different era. We upgrade panels and service entrance equipment to support modern loads.

EV Charging Stations — Multi-unit and commercial-grade EV charging for employee parking, customer lots, and multi-family properties. We handle design, permitting, and installation.

Emergency & Safety Work — Code violations, failing equipment, and urgent electrical issues that can't wait. We prioritize commercial clients with active safety concerns.
