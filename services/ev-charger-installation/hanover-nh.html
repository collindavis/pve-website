Why Upgrade to a Level 2 EV Charger?

A standard 120V outlet gives you 3–5 miles of range per hour. A Level 2 charger on a dedicated 240V circuit gives you 20–30 miles per hour. For most drivers, that means a full charge overnight — every night — regardless of how far you drove.

Every install includes a panel assessment, dedicated 240V/50-amp circuit, charger mounting and wiring, permit filing with We handle all permit filings with the Town of Hanover building department., and full testing before we leave. We work with all major brands including Tesla Wall Connector, ChargePoint, JuiceBox, Emporia, and Grizzl-E. Already have a charger? We'll install it. Need a recommendation? We'll help you choose.
